2004 - Present

Ask anyone who the greatest player in the NBA is. You'll likely hear the name Lebron James. In fact, you could have asked this question every year for the last 10 years, and still get the same response. It is without question that for over 15 years, Lebron has been at the top of his profession. Even in the 2003-2004 NBA season as a rookie, Lebron let the world know that it was his time, with stats similar to that of Michael Jordan's rookie numbers. Lebron would unsurprisingly go on to with the NBA Rookie Of The Year Award.
